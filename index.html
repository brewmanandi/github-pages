§ 3.1 — Service Fees: Technology Licensing

p. 4

The Licensor (Siemens AG, Germany) shall charge the Licensee (Siemens Ltd., Singapore) a royalty rate of 5.5% of net revenue§TP-R1 for use of the EW-500 industrial software platform. The rate is determined based on the comparable uncontrolled price (CUP) method§TP-M1, referencing third-party licenses entered into by the Licensor between 2021 and 2023.

Payment terms are net-60 from invoice date. The parties agree that the royalty rate shall be reviewed every two fiscal years§TP-C1 or following a material change in business circumstances, including but not limited to restructuring, significant revenue changes exceeding 20%, or regulatory changes in any jurisdiction where either party is tax-resident.

For the avoidance of doubt, the royalty base shall be defined as gross revenue less returns, discounts, and applicable indirect taxes. Documentation under OECD BEPS Action 13 requirements must be maintained contemporaneously.

§ 3.2 — Management Services

p. 5

Corporate management services shall be allocated using the cost-plus method§TP-M2 with a markup of 8.0–12.5%§TP-R2 over fully loaded costs. Allocation keys are headcount (40%), revenue (35%), and asset base (25%).

The allocation methodology is reviewed annually and documented in the intercompany allocation memorandum. For FY 2024, total management fees charged amounted to €8.7M§TP-F1, consistent with the prior year level adjusted for headcount growth of 6.2%.
